About SIS

SIS recognises that the client relationship is owned by the broker and that SIS has been entrusted by its brokers to provide a solution that supports the delivery of exceptional client service.

The underwriter-broker-client relationship is built on loyalty and trust and is intended to create long-term mutual benefit. Mutual benefit underpins SIS's purpose of building resilient communities where resilience is the ability of an individual or business to overcome adversity by leveraging the value of the relationships to which it is committed.

SIS views relational value creation as a product of doing what is right, acting with empathy, honesty, trust and making equitable decisions.

The SIS community extends to its reinsurers, brokers and their clients and includes the service providers that make the delivery its insurance products and services possible.

Through it's relationships, SIS has a positive influence.
